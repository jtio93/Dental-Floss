Frequently Asked Questions

Is this dentist-approved?
Absolutely! Fancy Floss was co-founded by Katie, a practicing dentist who graduated from Creighton University Dental School in 2020. Our mint-waxed floss is designed to be gentle on gums while effectively cleaning between teeth.
How do I refill the container?
Our container opens from the side for easy refilling. Simply pop it open, remove the empty spool, insert a new one, and you're good to go. It takes about 10 seconds!
What makes this different from drugstore floss?
Fancy Floss is made in Italy from premium materials that won't shred or get stuck between your teeth. Plus, our refillable design means you're not throwing away plastic containers every month. And it actually looks good on your bathroom counter.
Is the container really made from ocean-bound plastic?
Yes! Our containers are made from ocean-bound recycled plastic that was intercepted before it could reach our oceans, and they're designed to be used for years.
