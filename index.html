Our Story

It started at Dolores Park in San Francisco. My friend Katie and I were taking a break, enjoying the weather and a Mission burrito. Our conversation drifted to oral hygiene — as it does when you're friends with a dentist.

Katie graduated from Creighton University Dental School in 2020 and built her practice in San Francisco. I'm Jacquie — I have a biochemistry degree and spent years in quality control and quality assurance across biopharma, food production, and climate tech. When a piece of meat got stuck between my teeth, I asked Katie for her professional recommendation on the best floss available.

As serial flossers, we were both frustrated with what's out there. Most floss shreds. The sustainable options are expensive. Nothing really looked good sitting on the counter. We recognized the gap — and decided to make something better.

Katie knew what patients actually needed from her years treating them. My background in quality control — from pharmaceutical manufacturing to food safety to climate tech — gave me the foundation to understand what makes a product well-made. We chose to manufacture in Italy because of their commitment to quality craftsmanship and environmental standards.

So we made fancy floss. Thicker dry rise floss from Italy that reaches more surface area and cleans better than standard floss. A container made from ocean-bound recycled plastic that you'll actually want to refill. Designed in San Francisco, made with the environment in mind.

That's it. Just better floss from two friends who got tired of the options.
